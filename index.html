My name is Konstantin Kutzkov and I am working as a data science consultant. Despite working on industrial projects, I still maintain an active research profie. This page is dedicated to my research publications. You can visit my Linkedin profile for more details on my education and professional experience.

I obtained my PhD from ITU Copenhagen under the supervision of Rasmus Pagh on algorithmic techniques for data summarization. Most recently, my research interests have been in the area of graph machine learning. You can find my research papers below.  Also, I am describing the main ideas behind my research in blog posts intended for a more general audience, see my Medium profile.
